No Blog Posts

Recently there haven't been many posts on this blog. The reason is that I am working on several projects, but unfortunately none of them involves Grails or Groovy. But since I enjoy writing technical articles, I decided to extend the topic range of this blog with mobile application programming in Android.

Why Android?

The reason is, that all of my current projects are Android projects (the only exception is a tiny, single JEE project ;)) and I really feel there is a high-demand on good articles about mobile application patterns or best practices for Android developers. I swear to appropriately tag blog posts, if you have no interest in Android or related topics please subscribe to the Groovy, Grails or GContracts tag.
